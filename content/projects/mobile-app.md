# Mobile Banking App

## Overview

Designed and launched a mobile banking app that simplified financial management for over 50,000 users. The app focused on intuitive UX and seamless transaction experiences.

## Challenge

Traditional banking apps suffered from:
- Complex and confusing navigation
- Slow transaction processing
- Poor visibility into spending patterns
- Limited personalization options

## Approach

### Research & Discovery

Conducted extensive user research to understand pain points:
- 30+ user interviews
- Competitive analysis of 15 banking apps
- Usability testing with prototypes

### Design Principles

1. **Simplicity First**: Reduce cognitive load at every step
2. **Speed**: Optimize for quick transactions
3. **Transparency**: Clear feedback and status updates
4. **Personalization**: Adapt to user behavior

## Impact

- **50,000+ active users** within first year
- **4.8 star rating** on app stores
- **40% reduction** in customer support tickets
- **2x increase** in mobile transaction volume

## Technical Stack

- React Native (cross-platform)
- Node.js (backend)
- PostgreSQL (database)
- AWS (infrastructure)
